About Alexandria 🏖️

Alexandria, the Bride of the Mediterranean, combines natural beauty with rich history. Enjoy walking along the Corniche, explore the Bibliotheca Alexandrina, visit the fortress and museums, and wander through lively markets. Visiting Alexandria is a magical journey for anyone who loves culture and scenic beauty.
